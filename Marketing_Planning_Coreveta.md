# Marketing Strategy & Launch Plan: Coreveta

**Core Message**: "The Operational Kitchen for Your Service Business."

---

## 1. Market Positioning

Most technical service owners focus on "having a website." Coreveta pivots the conversation from "Digital Presence" to **"Operational Efficiency."** 

- **Hook**: Stop losing money on unrecorded parts and tardy technicians.
- **Narrative**: While others sell you a "storefront" (website), Coreveta provides the "kitchen" (management system) that actually makes the money.

## 2. Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)

1. **Automated Trust**: Every step is notified via WhatsApp (WAHA). Customers feel protected and informed.
2. **Accountability**: Real-time GPS and Photo verification for attendance. No more "ghost" visits.
3. **Inventory-to-Invoice**: Automatically deduct parts from stock and add them to bills. Zero leakage.
4. **Dashboard Control**: Owners can manage their business from a single "Command Center" screen.

## 3. Marketing Channels

### 3.1. Direct Sales (Inside Sales)
- **Target**: Medium-scale service shops (AC, CCTV, Electrical).
- **Tool**: Use the updated **Sales Bible** and **Chat Scripts**. Focus on the "Demo-first" approach to show the UI/UX.

### 3.2. Content Marketing (Educational)
- **Webinars/Short Videos**: "How to manage 50 AC technicians without headaches."
- **Case Studies**: Show before/after implementation of Coreveta (e.g., "Increased billing accuracy by 30%").

### 3.3. SEO Strategy
- Long-tail keywords: "Software manajemen servis AC," "Aplikasi teknisi lapangan terbaik," "Otomasi invoice WhatsApp."

### 3.4. Partnership
- Partner with tool suppliers or HVAC distributors. Offers Coreveta as a "Professionalization Tool" for their contractors.

## 4. Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Beta)
- **Audience**: 10 high-trust existing clients.
- **Goal**: Collect testimonials and identify minor UX friction.

### Phase 2: Public Launch
- **Action**: Full social media campaign and Cold Outreach.
- **Promotion**: "Free implementation & Onboarding" for the first 50 sign-ups.

### Phase 3: Scaling
- **Action**: Introduce "Referral commission" for users who bring in other business owners.

## 5. Pricing Strategy (Tiered)
